About

Hi, my name is Tess and I am pursuing a degree in digital design at AP College in Antwerp. Being creative has been a passion of mine throughout my life. Animation, handcrafting, and drawing are things I particularly find enjoyable. Till this day, I have kept my sketchbooks with me wherever I go. This is where my ideas, drawings and things that inspire me end up.

When I do something creative, I enjoy listening to a playlist that matches my mood. Sometimes I need full focus, but other times I like keeping things groovy and amusing while creating.

Speaking of keeping things groovy, I love to dance! Since I was young, this hobby has been a major part of my life. Dancing is my outlet to relief stress and think of nothing else but dance and have fun. This helps with clearing my head, which is beneficial for schoolwork.

Another small important thing I like to use, is a to-do list. Having a small note with everything I need to do everyday, from small tasks to big ones, provides me with a clear overview of things I still need to do. I am a perfectionist, so I like to have a summary of my work and projects.

Regarding that, because I love using my imagination, I like to come up with my own personal projects. I love exploring new ways to execute ideas and try other creative things that are new to me, or haven't tried yet. By doing this, I expand my knowledge of the creative world and learn to improve my work.
